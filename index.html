Training & Internship Program

Our training and internship programs are designed to provide a complete, immersive, and industry-focused learning experience. Students learn through expert-led live sessions with real-time interaction and doubt clarification, while recorded classes are made available through our LMS for easy revision anytime. We provide comprehensive study materials, including notes, reference guides, and curated resources, along with weekly assignments and mini-projects based on real-world development scenarios. Learners also benefit from monthly seminars and webinars conducted by industry professionals, keeping them updated with the latest technologies, trends, and career insights. As part of the program, students undergo a structured live internship where they work in teams on real client-based projects under dedicated mentors, gaining hands-on experience with wireframes, development workflows, client interactions, and task management using JIRA. Upon successful completion, students receive an official Internship Certificate and Experience Letter, along with dedicated placement support such as mock interviews, coding practice, resume building, and LinkedIn profile optimizationensuring they are job-ready and confident.
